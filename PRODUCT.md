# PRODUCT.md — Arca Nossa Senhora da Providência (ANSP)

## Register

**Brand.** The landing page (`design/index.html`) is a marketing/institutional surface: it communicates who the Arca is and routes families to the scholarship form. (The admin/form pages are a separate **product** surface.)

## Users & Purpose

- **Primary visitor:** a Catholic parent in Itatiba-SP considering enrolling a child at Colégio São José, often arriving because they need financial help (a bolsa).
- **Context:** likely on mobile, possibly anxious about cost, looking for trust and a clear next step.
- **Job to be done:** understand that the Arca is a serious, faith-aligned institution, then start a scholarship request.
- **Emotions to evoke:** institutional gravity with warmth — trust, dignity, reassurance. Never slick or commercial; this is a nonprofit rooted in faith and charity.

## Brand & Personality

Three words: **devout · dignified · providential.**

- Mantenedora (maintaining association) of Colégio São José, under the spiritual orientation of the FSSPX.
- Catholic-traditional. Latin-Mass tradition. The visual language is closer to a missal or a parish cornerstone than to a SaaS site.
- Warmth is carried by typography, the sacred imagery (Our Lady and the Christ Child), and copy — not by playfulness.

## Anti-references

- No startup/SaaS gloss: no gradient-mesh blobs, no hero-metric template, no emoji, no neon.
- Not a generic "church website" template (clip-art crosses, stocky smiling-family photos).
- Not editorial-magazine affectation for its own sake; the serif is institutional, not fashion.

## Strategic design principles

- **Identity is already committed** (see `docs/brand-spec.md`): navy + gold + warm paper, Iowan Old Style display serif, system sans body, the circular ARCA seal. Preserve it — variants don't second-guess shipping identity.
- **Sacred imagery is required, not optional.** A Catholic institution page with zero imagery reads as incomplete. Use the real assets: `logo-gesso.jpg` (cream gesso statue of Our Lady + Child on navy — matches the palette) and `base1.png`/`base2.png` (Murillo-style paintings).
- **One clear conversion path:** "Faça sua solicitação de bolsa" → `formulario.html`.
- **Accessibility:** pt-BR, body contrast ≥4.5:1, respects `prefers-reduced-motion`, keyboard-navigable.
